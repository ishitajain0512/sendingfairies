What is Primary Care?

A primary care doctor is the best first point of contact for all of your health needs. Primary care doctors are trained to diagnose and treat a wide variety of health problems. Their specialty is keeping you healthy. A primary care doctor can:

  • Diagnose and help you manage chronic conditions, like asthma, diabetes and high blood pressure
  • Make sure you get the preventive services you need, like routine screenings and immunizations
  • Refer you to qualified specialists as needed
  • Support you in reaching your personal wellness goals
